The Elements of Style Redesign

I redesigned the popular book, The Elements of Style, by William Strunk Jr. My approach was a sleek and modern design with unique elements, like the lasercut chapter pages, to delight the reader. The book is printed with the typefaces Adobe Caslon Pro and Avenir. I handmade and bound it myself.
