Amor & Explore Magazine

Amor and explore magazine combines the act of traveling with romance. Stories and articles are focused on adventures and benefits of traveling as a couple. Though it started out as a print piece, Amor & Explore has expanded into a responsive, online magazine. The online version allows you the user to access a wider variety of destinations to learn about, as well as view popular activities in those areas. Viewers are encouraged to submit their own stories and personal adventures on the website, as well as in the print magazine.
