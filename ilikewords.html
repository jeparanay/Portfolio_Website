I Like Words

I Like Words and Pronunciation combines Robert Pirosh’s letter, “I Like Words,” and the popular poem, “The Chaos,” by Gerard Nolst Trenité. The two texts come together to help one better understand the complex and confusing English Language, showing that when words are properly understood, they can be fun to use, and make a bold statement. Robert Pirosh’s “I Like Words,” aids this idea; he submitted this letter as a job application, which landed his dream job with MGM as a screenwriter.
