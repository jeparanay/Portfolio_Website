First Things First

These posters demonstrate my opinion of the First Things First manifesto, written by Ken Garland. They show that the consumerist culture feeds us lies on a daily basis, and more honest designers are needed. My posters, accompanied by the short video, bring awareness to this problem in an effort to overcome it. I designed and produced the cereal boxes and magazine covers.
