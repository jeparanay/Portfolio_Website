Visual Database

In my free time, one of my favorite things to do is watch movies. For this infographic, I chose to visually organize the first 100 movies I could remember into 7 categories. The categories were based on my personal impression of the movies. Someone viewing my infographic can choose a movie based on the information given through my database.
