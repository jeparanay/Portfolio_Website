About Liaison International

Liaison International helps colleges with their admissions process by providing them with resources to make their lives as well as their students lives easier. Our Applicant portal is used by colleges across the country. It allows students applying for Masters and some undergraduate programs to search and apply to multiple programs at once using the same application for all their programs.

Example of Specs for Development

Compiled Specs from my Style Guide

I created this Spec Component sheet to make it easy for designers to grab from when specing their designs. The styles are based off the style guide I created for Liaison. I found that designers were creating multiple pages of specs for one page of a design and handing them off to the developers. This approach was confusing and caused the developers to have to look through multiple pages for one design. My spec components allow designers to put all the specs on one page. This not only sped up the process for us as designers, but the developers found it to be more clear, concise, and understandable.
