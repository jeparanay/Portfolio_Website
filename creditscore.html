Understanding Credit Score

Financial education is hard to come by when you're young. I was lucky enough to have a parent who worked in finance, and was able to learn important information at a young age. My mom taught me the basics of establishing good credit, but I noticed that most of my peers didn't have the same luck and didn't have much time to invest in learning.

To solve for this problem and share my knowledge with my peers, I decided to create a quick game that covers all the basics of your credit score. While clicking through some brief scenarios, you are prompted to decide whether your credit score will increase, decrease, or stay the same. It's a quick and easy way to discover some hidden secrets about credit and things you do that effect your score.
