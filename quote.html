The Fidelity Quote Section

During our re-design phase of the Fidelity Mobile App, I lead the design for the Quote section. The process for re-designing this section included: meeting with the business team to figure out project goals and requirements for each quote type, bringing concepts to final designs, building out prototypes to test specific interactions, delivering specs to development, and working with the developers to deliver a pixel perfect product. Shown here are the designs for Equity Quotes, but check out the Fidelity Mobile App by downloading it on your mobile device to view the other quote designs.
