Eat Here

With so many options, its not always easy to choose a restaurant, especially when you're out with a group who all have different requests. Eat Here is a community based app that provides you with insider tips from locals to help you decide where to eat. A quick questionnaire will help you weed through restaurants to find the perfect place.

Helps you decide where to eat.

Provides you with insider tips.

Ensures you have a great meal.

Around Me

Restaurants around you can be filtered by distance, price, food type, and atmosphere. Map view allows you to see exactly where the restaurants are, and get some insider tips. List view displays a bit more information, including the restaurant's hours as well as the insider tips.

Questionnaire

By filling out the brief questionnaire you can quickly choose where to eat. Questions refering to group size, atmosphere, food type, food alergies, and time of day help you find the perfect restaurant to suit all of your needs.

Restaurant Page

In a specific restaurant's view, you can see information, insider tips, photography, and their menu. If you decide a restaurant isn't for you, it's easy to view other restaurants close by.
