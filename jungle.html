Jungle Web Design

Jungle hired me to help them with their vision for their website. My main focus was to create a vector graphic for mobile and desktop that would be displayed on their home page. The graphic represents the vibe of their company. Links to other sections of their website are hidden within the billboard like areas of the graphic.
