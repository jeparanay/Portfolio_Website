The Fidelity Feed

After my internship turned into a full time position on the Mobile Team in the User Experience Design department, it was decided that we were going to begin a complpete redesign of the Fidelity Mobile App. From the very beginning I took part in creative visioning, concept development, and mocking up our plans for the new experience. The different sections of the app were divided up amongst the team, and one of my undertakings was the new home screen - The Feed.

As design lead for the feed, I met with the business team to figure out project goals and requirements, brought concepts through sketches to final designs, worked out the functionality and look and feel of the experience, built out prototypes to test specific interactions, delivered specs to development, and worked with the developers until the feed was ready for launch.

The feed is an ongoing project, we are always looking to better the experience. Check it out by downloading the app on your mobile device.
