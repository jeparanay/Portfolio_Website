Hello,

I'm Julia, a recent graduate from MassArt with a BFA in Graphic Design. My passion for design stems from my love of adventures, games, challenges, and technology. Solving design problems allows me to channel all of these interests while expressing my creativity as I find the best possible solution to the challenge at hand. I believe design should be useful, meaningful, and beautiful, and I understand the process it takes to get there. Mobile and Web are what inspire me most, but I also enjoy print based media.

Feel free to reach out, I'd love to hear from you.
