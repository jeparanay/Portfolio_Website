Hello! I'm Julia.

Visual/UX Designer & Front-End Developer

My passion for design stems from my love of adventures, games, challenges, and technology. Solving design problems allows me to channel all of these interests while expressing my creativity as I find the best possible solution to the challenge at hand. I believe design should be useful, meaningful, and beautiful, and I understand the process it takes to get there. I graduated from MassArt with a BFA in Graphic Design, and I've been working in the field ever since. Web design and Mobile design are what inspire me most these days, but I also enjoy print.
