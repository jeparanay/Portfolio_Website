Wedding Cake Box Display

I created this large Wedding Cake box to accompany pre-existing cake flatware packaging in the showroom at Lifetime Brands. The flatware boxes, shaped as cake slices, each held a cake knife and server. The cake box gives context to the individual cake knife and server boxes when on display. Dimensions: 1' 4" tall and 2' 4" wide.
