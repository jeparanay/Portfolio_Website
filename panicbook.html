Music Box Set

To this day, I have yet to become bored with any songs from Panic At The Disco's albums. For this piece, I showed the progression and changes in their music from album to album through color, and doodles. My hand doodles can be found in every notebook I own, and are always accompanied by lyrics to these songs. This has been how I express myself through their music, so I used this act of doodling throughout my book to enhance the feel of each album.
