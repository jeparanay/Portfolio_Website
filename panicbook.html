Music Box Set

To this day, I have yet to become bored with any songs from Panic At The Disco's albums. This is due to their music style shift from album to album. I showed this progression through color, hand lettering, and hand sketches. My hand sketches and lettering reflect the experience of listening to the unique lyrics of Panic's songs. The box set went through two design iterations before landing at the final product. Featured in the Senior Show at MassArt, April 2015
