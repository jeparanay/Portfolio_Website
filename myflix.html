MyFlix

MyFlix allows you to keep track of the movies you have watched or movies that you want to watch. As you add movies to your lists, you can rate, share, and discuss them with your friends. Discover a movie you might want to watch with a friend? It is easy to share it on their profile, or even request to watch it with them! If you're an avid movie watcher, MyFlix is the app for you.
