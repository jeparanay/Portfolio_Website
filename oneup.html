The One Up Experience

We all have goals, and the intention of having these goals is to actively make our life better. By working on the things that we want to accomplish, we are putting ourselves first, and doing something that will make us happier later. Although this seems like a great plan, it's not always that simple to fufill your goals.

Visualizing Goals with Augmented Reality

Along the way there are things that distract us, and it's easy to give up when you don't see an instant reward for staying true to your goal. The One Up Experience adds gamification to your everyday life, and seamlessly keeps track of your goals using Augmented Reality Contact Lenses and the One Up app.

Reaching your goals through Gamification

After setting up a goal, simply send the challenge to a friend and wait for an approval. The challenge can be:

  • Bet based (Compete for Money)
  • Object based (Compete for an Object)
  • Action based (Compete for an Action ex. a chore)

Adding competitive game elements to the process of reaching a goal allows you to be rewarded and have a reason to continue working towards the end result.

All your data entered for you

After your goals have been set up, there is no need to put information into the app. The Augmented Reality Contact lenses use image recognition to keep up with your progress. One Up makes your goals your reality.
