Motional

Working at Motional since early 2021, I've grown my career and touched many aspects of our driverless experience. The redesign of our Command Center, a tool which our operations teams use to oversee our fleet of autonomous vehicles, was my first undertaking. This project work consisted of researching user needs with our ops team, creating and maintaining a new design system, leading the design of many key features, and working cross teams on other tools and projects.

In Feburary of 2023, I took on a new role leading a high stakes team which oversees key functionality of our autonomous vehicles. In addition to leading design of new features, I've created another design system with new and improved components, implemented new processes for onboarding new team members as we expand, and have begun learning 3D modeling.

If you'd like to learn more about my role in this project, my process, or want to work together - send me a message below!
